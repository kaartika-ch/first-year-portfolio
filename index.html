PROJECT DETAILS
1. THE BRIEF ↓

Based on the design brief, I will understand the project requirements and goals. This will help in developing a realistic timeline and ensures the potential to find good design solutions.

2. RESEARCH & ANALYSIS ↓

Every good design is based on detailed research. Analysing the brief, the product, and competitors helps identify the primary user needs and potential challenges early in the design process. This supports me developing the best possible creative decisions for the project.

3. IDEATION ↓

In the brainstorming stage there is no good or bad idea. The aim is to put down as many ideas as possible that address the problem statement. All my thoughts are put down in a sketchbook and evaluated later to identify the best ones.

4. PROTOTYPING ↓

Thumbnails, iterations, and mockups are generated. This is where I try out different colour palettes, experiment with other design choices until I find the ones I'm satisfied with.

5. FINAL DESIGN ↓

Before I approach this stage, I usually get enough feedback about the work so that I'm able to produce my best work. However, even after developing the final design, there are usually things which need to be changed to ensure the design is polished to its fullest potential.
