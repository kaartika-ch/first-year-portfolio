EAT LIKE A LOCAL IN HYDERABAD:
Brochure Design

For my brochure, I decided to revist the streets of Hyderabad. The dishes explored are unique to the city, and the restaurants mentioned are must-try spots in the city. Overall, this brochure is a testament to the food culture in Hyderabad.

[ 1 ] THE CHALLENGE

To create a tri-fold brochure based on research on the Food & Beverage industry in any city of your choice. The brochure is to guide a visitor through the food and beverage culture and hotspots in town.

[ 2 ] THE BASICS

A brochure design based on the research and understanding of a Hyderabad's food and beverage culture.

[ 3 ] THE DETAILS

Tools: Adobe Photoshop + InDesign
Time Spent: 2 Weeks

[ 4 ] THE PROCESS

I began by speaking to local foodies in Hyderabad, and reading food blogs and critic reviews online. This helped me compile a list of must-try restaurants and dishes.
